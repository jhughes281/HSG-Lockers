How It Works

Simple for Everyone — Powerful Behind the Scenes

Our lockers come pre-loaded with software and are ready to use immediately. Full third-party software integration is also available.

01

Courier Creates Account & Logs In

Delivery driver creates an account on the touchscreen, selects the recipient, and chooses an available locker size.

02

Package Placed & Door Secured

Driver places the package inside the selected compartment. The system auto-assigns and locks the door upon closing.

03

Resident Notified Instantly

Recipient receives an SMS and/or email with a unique QR code or PIN to retrieve their package at any time.

01

Customer Orders Online

Customer places a food order through the restaurant's app or website and selects locker pickup at checkout.

02

Staff Places Order in Locker

Kitchen staff selects the right temperature compartment, places the order inside, and closes the door to complete.

03

Customer Scans & Picks Up

Customer arrives, scans their QR code from their phone, the door pops open automatically, and they grab their order.

01

Employee Authenticates

Worker scans their RFID badge, enters a PIN, or uses biometric authentication at the locker kiosk to access the system.

02

Asset Checked Out

The assigned compartment opens automatically. The system logs the employee, timestamp, and asset with full traceability.

03

Return & Real-Time Tracking

When the asset is returned, it's logged back in. Managers see real-time dashboards showing asset location, usage, and availability.
