Service Tiers

Flexible pickup
on your schedule

Property managers set the schedule once. Residents get door-to-door convenience. HSG handles every pickup, route, and removal — one contract, one contact.

01
Weekly
Standard Weekly Pickup

Door-to-door trash collection on a fixed day each week. Property manager sets the route — residents set out their bin, we do the rest.

  • Fixed collection day per property
  • Resident door-to-door service
  • Transport to on-site dumpster
  • Resident pickup reminders included
02
Bi-Weekly
Bi-Weekly Pickup

A lighter-footprint plan for smaller properties or lower-volume communities. Same trusted door-to-door service — every two weeks.

  • Every 14 days, same day each cycle
  • Ideal for smaller complexes
  • Flexible upgrade to weekly anytime
  • Same crew and route consistency
03
Monthly · Heavy Items
Monthly Heavy Item Removal

Furniture, appliances, large boxes. Residents schedule a pickup window within a designated monthly day. Available curbside or in-unit by appointment.

  • One scheduled removal day per month
  • Resident-chosen pickup window
  • Curbside or in-unit options
  • Furniture, appliances, bulk items
